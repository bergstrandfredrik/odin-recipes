Bacon Pancakes

Description

These bacon pancakes are the sweet-salty combo that will start your day out right.

It's a breakfast all in one, with plenty of bacon in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Sugar
  • flour
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour and sugar.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan.
  3. Turn dough out onto a work surface and knead for 1 minute.
